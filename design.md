All colors are defined as CSS custom properties in the `:root` block at the top of `styles.css`.

## Brand Colors
- `--color-primary: #5A84D8` — Buttons, accents, links, topic boxes
- `--color-primary-hover: #4a72c4` — Button hover states
- `--color-nav-cta: #3B6BDE` — Nav CTA button
- `--color-nav-cta-hover: #2f59c4` — Nav CTA hover

## Dark Backgrounds
- `--color-dark-navy: #12254f` — Top bar, footer
- `--color-hero-dark-1: #2a2b2f` — Hero gradient start
- `--color-hero-dark-2: #36373b` — Hero gradient mid
- `--color-hero-dark-3: #2e3348` — Hero gradient end

## Text Colors
- `--color-text: #333` — Body text, headings
- `--color-text-article: #444` — Article paragraph text
- `--color-text-muted: #555` — Labels, contact info
- `--color-dark-title: #1f2d48` — CTA card headings

## Backgrounds
- `--color-bg-light: #f0f0f0` — Section backgrounds
- `--color-bg-cta-card: #f3f4f6` — CTA card background
